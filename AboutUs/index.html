About Us

Introduction to Lucena City Library and Libraprint

Lucena City Library stands as a cornerstone of public knowledge, offering residents, students, and researchers access to a wide range of learning resources and community-driven programs. As part of its commitment to modernization and improved service delivery, the library has partnered with student as developers and researchers to introduce LibraPrint an innovative library management system designed to enhance library operations through automation, analytics, and intelligent features.

Mission and Vision

Mission

LibraPrint aims to modernize and simplify public library operations by providing an intelligent, user-friendly, and data-driven management system. Through the integration of barcode tracking, biometric attendance, and descriptive analytics, LibraPrint empowers librarians to efficiently manage resources, improve user experience, and support transparent reporting to national institutions.

Vision

To become a leading model of digital transformation in Philippine public libraries by fostering innovation, operational excellence, and data-informed decision-making ensuring accessible, organized, and technology-enabled library services for every citizen.
